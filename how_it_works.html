Your Story. Your way.

Our expertise in multimedia production and storytelling can encompass all your life’s defining moments. And our creative juices are already flowing to start producing and developing an innovative, artistically compelling and meaningful story.

5Simple steps to get your story told:

  • FREE CONSULTATION

    Complete the Contact Form. Within 24 hours a production team member will contact you to schedule a no cost, no obligation telephone consultation to listen to your needs, story, and better understand your storytelling needs.

  • CONTENT SELECTION

    Select and organize your content (e.g., photos, videos, images, audio, text, etc.) while considering what story you want to tell. We’ll be available to advise you about creative and artistic presentation techniques. These techniques will ensure that your story is presented in a compelling, memorable, and meaningful way. Get Ideas. Get Inspired.

  • CONTENT TRANSFER

    Send your content to us. There are various options for transferring your content for production (e.g., online, mail), which will be determined during the free consultation call.

  • REVIEW PROCESS

    We believe your voice and ideas are critical in the storytelling and production process. Therefore, based on the complexity of your project, you’ll have preview opportunities before final approval.

  • VIEWING OPTIONS

    A completed VideoStory can be enjoyed by viewing on various devices (e.g., TV, DVD player, computer, tablet, smartphone, etc.). Therefore, the VideoStory will be formatted to meet your viewing needs (e.g., online streaming, video projection during event, DVD, TV, etc.).

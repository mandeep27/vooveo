Long-form videos are designed to be viewed at an event or given as a gift. With your guests or someone special in mind, a VideoStory or VideoDocument can be an original way to revisit and share those captured moments shared together.

With the RealTime Social feature, your guests can interact and engage by taking photos and videos at your event and then your guests’ photos and videos can be incorporated into your final VideoStory.
